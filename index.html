Intro

Hi, I'm Melissa Aoko, a dedicated and enthusiastic student at the University of Calgary, pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. With a deep passion for technology and innovation, I am on a journey to explore the vast world of computer science, from coding and algorithms to software development and beyond.

On this website, you’ll find an insight into my academic and personal growth as a software engineer. Here’s a glimpse of what you can explore:

  • About Me: Learn more about my background, interests, and goals in the field of computer science.
  • Projects: Discover the projects I am working on, my ideas for future projects, and the skills I am developing.
  • Contact: Get in touch with me by connecting with me on social media.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you find my journey as inspiring as it is exciting.

Growing up in Kenya, I always had a strong desire to expand the knowledge and use of artificial intelligence and computer technology. I envisioned a future where technology could drive innovation and improve the quality of life in my community and beyond. This aspiration fueled my passion for coding and my commitment to making a positive impact through technology.

At the University of Calgary, I am fortunate to be surrounded by a vibrant community of learners and educators who inspire me every day. Through my coursework and projects, I have developed a strong foundation in various programming languages and technologies, and I am constantly seeking to expand my knowledge and skills.

Beyond academics, I am involved in a few student organizations and tech communities. These experiences have not only enhanced my technical abilities but also helped me develop leadership and teamwork skills. I enjoy collaborating with others on projects and learning from their perspectives.

My goal is to use my skills and knowledge to create innovative solutions that can make a positive impact on society. I am particularly interested in developing applications that address environmental and social challenges. I believe that technology has the power to change the world, and I am eager to be a part of that change.

Thank you for visiting my website. I hope you find my projects and aspirations inspiring. Feel free to reach out to me if you have any questions, collaboration ideas, or just want to connect!
